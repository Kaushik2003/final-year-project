This approach integrates modular technologies to ensure that an operator has reliable, high-performance tools in the most demanding combat environments.

    Ruggedized Display (AR): Leveraging partnerships with experts like Oakley Standard Issue, Meta, and Gentex_Corp, the system incorporates advanced AR display technology housed within ballistic-rated eyewear.
